Magic Items

Heward's Handy Hand Grenade

Wonderous Item, Very Rare.

Once per turn you can use your action to throw a hand grenade. The grenade splits into two parts, the magical part, and a hand grenade with the pin pulled. It explodes on this initiative count next turn. You can throw the grenade a maximum of 4*strength feet. Any creature within 5 feet takes 2d12 piercing damage, and any within 15 feet takes 1d10+4 thunder damage.

Whip of Imprisonment

Weapon (Whip), Very Rare

This whip has a +2 to attack and damage rolls, a range of 20 feet, and on a hit the target takes 1d8 extra bludgeoning damage. If you score a critical hit upon a target who is huge or smaller in size you can restrain it untill you choose to release it. If the whip is restraining someone you can make no further attacks with the whip until you release the target.
