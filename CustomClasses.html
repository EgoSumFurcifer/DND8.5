Draconist

A lizardfolk with red scales stands in front of a hoard and releases his inner fire and burns the group to ashes. A goliath slinks through the shadows, following a party of corrupt noblemen, and with a suprise attack all the noblemen fall in a cloud of darkness that emerged from the goliath's mouth. An elf with gleaming scales and two scimitars whirls through the orc encampment, downing foe after foe before stopping and unleashing a sparkling cloud that puts all the orcs to sleep. A draconist learns the powers of dragons, and adopts those powers into their own body and form. They combine the art of finesse weaponry and draconic gifts to wreak havoc on the battlefield at both short and long ranges.

Class Features

As a draconist, you gain the following class features.

Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1d10 per draconist level
Hit Points at 1st Level: 10 + Constitution modifier
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per draconist level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: Light, Medium
Weapons: Ranged weapons, Daggers, Rapiers, Scimitars, Shortswords, Whips
Tools: None
Saving Throws: Constitution, Dexterity
Skills: Choose two from Insight, Perception, Persuasion, Animal Handling, and History.

Fighting Style

Archery - You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls you make with ranged weapons. Dueling - When you are wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons, you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls with that weapon. Two-Weapon Fighting - When you engage in two-weapon fighting, you can add your ability modifier to the damage of the second attack.

Tongue of the Dragon

At 1st level you learn the language of the dragons. You gain Draconic as a language, if you dont already have it.

Draconic Defense

At 1st level your skin becomes hard and scaled. When you are wearing no armor, your AC equals 13 + your dexterity modifier. You can use a shield and still gain the benifits of this feature.
When you reach higher levels in this class the base AC increases: 14 at 6th level, 15 at 13th level, and 16 at 17th level.

Breath Weapon

When you reach 2nd level in this class your affinity with dragons grants you a portion of their detructive breath. Once during your turn when you take the attak action, you can attack using your breath weapon, you can force everybody in an area to make a saving throw (DC=8+Proficiency Bonus+Constitution Modifier) or take your constitution modifier number of d4 damage. You regain all uses after a short or long rest.
You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, but can only use it once per turn, even if you can attack more then once.
The damage dice increases to d6 at 5th level, d8 at 8th level, d10 at 11th level, d12 at 14th level, and d20 at 18th level, and the area of effect becomes a 20 foot cone at 5th level, 25 foot cone at 8th level, 30 foot cone at 11th level, 35 foot cone at 14th level,and a 40 foot cone at 18th level.

Draconic Path

When you reach 2nd level in this class, you choose a draconic path to follow. Choose from Path of Shadow, Path of the Arcane Dragon, Path of the Chromatic Dragon, and Path of the Metallic Dragon. Your choice grants you features at 2nd, 7th, 10th, and 15th levels/
